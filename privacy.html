Privacy Policy for Hassang

Effective date: April 1, 2026

Hassang is a fitness competition app that lets users track workouts, earn points, and view rankings on leaderboards.

Information We Collect

Hassang may collect and store the following information:

  • Name or username
  • Email address used for login
  • Profile photo, if you choose to upload one
  • Location information such as country, state, and city
  • Workout data such as plank time and running distance/time

Camera Permission

Hassang uses the camera for workout-related features such as plank form detection. Camera access is only used for app functionality.

Location Permission

Hassang uses location permission for running tracking and leaderboard location features.

How We Use Data

Your data is used to:

  • Provide workout tracking features
  • Save workout progress and stats
  • Show rankings on leaderboards
  • Personalize your profile
  • Support app functionality and improvement

Data Storage

Data may be stored using Firebase services to support authentication, profile information, and workout statistics.

Ads

Hassang may display ads. Advertising services may collect limited technical information as needed to serve ads in accordance with their own policies.

Data Sharing

Hassang does not sell your personal data. Data is only used as needed for app functionality and related services.

Security

We take reasonable steps to protect user information, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Your Choices

You may choose whether to upload a profile photo or provide location details. You can also stop using the app at any time.
